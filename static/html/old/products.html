BaaS
Badges as a Service is the only product that we are scheduling to release in beta at this time.

Summary
Many websites and online services have started using badges (or trophies) to provide incentives for their users, this trend will continue to grow, and could be the key for many online services in retaining important membership. GameInfuzer's BaaS is a way for websites to award users attractive badges, and render those badges on users' profile pages (or posts, comments, replies, etc).

An illustration and use case is outline below:

Use case
Say site example.com is a "question & answer" website that wants to provide incentive to their users. They may want to award users for asking questions, and award users for answering questions. The site may want to award users that ask good questions (ones that spark a lot of discussion), and the site may also want to award users that answer questions frequently and/or accurately.

The company from this example, example.com, could use GameInfuzer as the award managing service. Rather than building the awarding service from scratch, example.com would use our simple API to reward badges to users, and render awarded badges per user on user profile pages.
